# Selected Topic

Your AI Agent Needs a QA Contract, Not More Prompts

## Core Angle

Most teams are trying to fix unreliable AI agents by writing longer prompts. The better QA move is to define a contract: instructions, context, skills, rules, expected outputs, refusal paths, and observable checks. This connects directly to ICSR, AI Tests AI, Pattern Recognition, Quality Gates, and Workflow.

## One-Line Thesis

If your agent can create Jira tickets, run Playwright tests, or touch production data, it needs a QA contract before it needs another prompt rewrite.
